How it works

Installation: Easily install Desknote on your system with just a few simple commands.

Create Notes: Open Desknote in your terminal and start creating notes using intuitive commands.

Organize: Keep your notes organized by adding tags or categories for quick access.

Edit and Update: Effortlessly modify and update your notes as your ideas evolve.

Search Function: Find specific notes quickly with a powerful search feature.

Stay Productive: Enjoy a distraction-free environment to focus on what matters most.
